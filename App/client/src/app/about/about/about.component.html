Quacklity

Quacklity empowers people to enjoy alcoholic beverages to the fullest

The Quacklity website is a place where you can find the best alcoholic beverage for the right moment. Starting from displaying the top-rated beverages at the home component with an option to skip through beverage types. Comfortable shop design with a quick filtering to help you pick the best beverage for you at the right moment. Convinient bottle design All the way to the checkout component where you can manage your final choices. Start rating bottles to help other users pick the right beverage and start a community of followers to empower people know better about alcohol.

Who are we?

So who we are? This website developed by now by one junior developer based on his personal taste of making a comfortable platform for users to find their favorite beverage for the right moment and help them discover new beverages tastes. The website driven by the users where they rate other bottles and purchase them so other people can discover new beverages based on who are they following.
